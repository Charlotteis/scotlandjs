Accommodation

Edinburgh has a wealth of accommodation options to suit all budgets.

If you would like to be close to Summerhall you can find the Marriott Residence Inn or the Best Western about a 10 minute walk away.

Those wishing to be in the heart of the Old Town can check out the Ibis Hotel or Jurys Inn. Both are around a 25 minute walk to ScotlandJS.

There are also a number of Travelodges around the city which are less expensive.

A full list of options, including Bed and Breakfast accommodation, are available at LateRooms.com.
