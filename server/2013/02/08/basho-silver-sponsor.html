Basho - Silver Sponsor

posted by Mark Connell

We are pleased to announce that Basho are a silver sponsor this year:

Basho is the company behind Riak: the open source, scalable, highly available, fault tolerant key-value database that's ideal for your next web-scale project. It's in use at Rovio, AT&T, the Danish national health authority and at many other high profiles organisations. There are clients for Node.js, Ruby, Python, Java, Erlang and many other environments. Basho recently opened a London office and are building the Riak community throughout Europe.
