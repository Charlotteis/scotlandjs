Those lovely people at GitHub have kindly sponsored ScotlandJS for the second year running!

GitHubber, Coby Chapple, is keen to make sure that this is a ScotlandJS that you do not forget and is very kindly hosting Edinburghs first GitHub DrinkUp.

Of course this is open to everyone, not just ScotlandJS delegates, so get yourself to the Royal Dick bar within Summerhall for 7:30pm on Friday the 10th of May!
