The kind folks over at Fuzzy Orange and Scotch on the Rocks are giving away their ScotlandJS ticket!

To be in with a chance of winning the 2 day conference ticket all you have to do is sign up to our NodeCopter event.

On Friday the 26th of May, Fuzzy Orange will randomly select one of the sign ups on the NodeCopter JustGiving page to gift their ScotlandJS ticket to.

So one lucky person will get to goto NodeCopter, donate money to Maggie's Cancer Centres and get a free ScotlandJS ticket!
