Call for Proposals

Here at ScotlandJS we have one criteria for your talk submission(s); that your talk be on a subject that you are passionate about.

Simple as that. Submit your proposal now.

What you need to know:

  • Talks will be 20 minutes long.
  • Talks are followed by a 20 minutes discussion held in the parallel track to allow attendees to dig deeper.
  • CFP closes on Sunday 15 February 2015 at 23:59 GMT.
  • Ticket holders blind vote on submissions.
  • All submissions and presentations must adhere to our Code of Conduct.
  • Unfortunately we cannot cover speakers expenses. However we want to help speakers from minority groups by offering up to £200 towards travel and accomodation expenses.
