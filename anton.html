Anton Whalley

Realtime replication in the realworld with node.js

@antonwhalley

Bio

VP Engineering at Nearform. Love talking about node.js Hardware hacking and websockets

Talk

There have been some fantastic advances in the node.js and javascript communities over the past year in terms of replication and p2p networks.

Unfortunately most of the work is still a little inaccessible and realworld examples that tease out some of the main challenges that could befall a development team are still a little light on the ground.

I am currently working on projects that are meeting these challanges and I would love to discuss some of the work that I have been doing for clients in this space and also talk about some interesting options that developers could bring to their own organisations.
