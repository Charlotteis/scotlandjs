Charity Hack Day

Each year we prominent members of the JavaScript community give their time and experience to share knowledge and help raise funds for Maggie's Cancer Centres.

You can secure your ticket by donating at least £50 on our JustGiving page.

Web Audio Hack Day

On Wednesday the 6th of May join us for an entire day of hacking on the Web Audio API and the Web MIDI API sponsored by CodeBase

We will start the day by giving a short introduction on the Web Audio API, the Web MIDI API, debuggers and other important basics. After that the hacking can begin! Programmers that are more experienced with the API can start hacking right away. You can work on projects in groups or on your own. If you don't know what to hack on we have a list of interesting experiments to get you motivated!

After several hours of hacking (approx between 4pm and 5pm) we will present all projects in the evening on the big screen.

Maggie's Cancer Centres

Maggie's provides free practical, emotional and social support to people with cancer and their family and friends, following the ideas about cancer care originally laid out by Maggie Keswick Jencks.

Built in the grounds of NHS cancer hospitals, Maggie's Centres are places with professional staff on hand to offer the support people need.

Our Centres are places to find practical advice about benefits and eating well; places where qualified experts provide emotional support; places to meet other people; places where you can simply sit quietly with a cup of tea.

The first Maggie's Centre opened in Edinburgh in 1996 and since then Maggie's has continued to grow, with 17 Centres at major NHS cancer hospitals in the UK, online and abroad.
