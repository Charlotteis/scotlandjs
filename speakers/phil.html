--- layout: speaker title: Phil Leggetter speaker_name: Phil Leggetter --- We all agree that having well tested apps is important, right? In an agile environment it gives you the confidence to iteratively refactor as requirements change. And the larger your application, the more important this becomes. Not only does it promote quality, but also a scalable application architecture that's essential when building \"Ginormous JavaScript Apps\".\r\n\r\nIn this talk I'll discuss and demonstrate how testing has been core to [Caplin Systems](http://www.caplin.com) successfully building and maintaining a **100k+ LoC front-end JavaScript** SDK and multiple 50k+ LoC apps. In particular, the application architecture required to enable testing apps of this size.
