Call for Proposals

Here at ScotlandJS we have one criteria for your talk submission(s); that your talk be on a subject that you are passionate about.

It can be anything related to developing with JavaScript be that client side, server side, WebAudio, CSS, UX, experience reports or development process.

Simple as that. Submit your proposal now.

What you need to know:

  • Talks will be 20 minutes long.
  • Talks are followed by a 20 minutes discussion held in the parallel track to allow attendees to dig deeper.
  • CFP closes on Sunday 15 February 2015 at 23:59 GMT.
  • Ticket holders blind vote on submissions.
  • All submissions and presentations must adhere to our Code of Conduct.
  • Unfortunately we cannot cover speakers expenses. However we want to help speakers from minority groups by offering up to £200 towards travel and accomodation expenses.

This sounds great, I've got questions:

We'll be hosting two online video chat sessions for potential speakers to give you the opportunity to find out more about ScotlandJS. To help field questions we have drafted in two previous speakers that have been though this process before, Jo Liss and Philip Roberts!

Both sessions will be on talky.io and you'll need Chrome or Firefox to join in.

  1. Sunday 1st February at 8pm GMT until 9pm.
  2. and Wednesday 4th February at 8pm GMT until 9pm here.

Details of the chat sessions will be announced here.

If you would prefer to email or chat one-to-one feel free to get in touch here.

If you want to dive right in get started by submitting your proposal.

We look forward to hearing from you!
